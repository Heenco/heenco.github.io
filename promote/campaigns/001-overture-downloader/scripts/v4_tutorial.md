# TUTORIAL — v4
> Hook: Get global building data for your thesis study area — free, in 60 seconds.
> Platform: tiktok-9x16 | Duration: 45s | Audience: researcher-student

[SECTION 1 — HOOK 0:00–0:05]
If you need spatial data for your research and you don't have an ArcGIS license — listen up.

[SECTION 2 — WHAT IT IS 0:05–0:15]
Overture Maps is a free, open dataset backed by Amazon, Microsoft, and Meta. Global buildings, roads, POIs, addresses. All free. But downloading it normally requires Python or SQL.

[SECTION 3 — THE TOOL 0:15–0:35]
I built a browser tool — the Overture Downloader. You go to heenco.com/tools/overture-downloader, draw a box around your study area on the map, pick your dataset — let's say Places — and hit download. You get a GeoJSON file you can open in QGIS, R, or Python instantly.

[SECTION 4 — CTA 0:35–0:45]
No account. No cost. No install. Link in bio — and yes, it works for international areas too.
