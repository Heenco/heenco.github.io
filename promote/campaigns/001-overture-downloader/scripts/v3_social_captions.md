## LinkedIn
If you've tried to access Overture Maps data, you know there's a gap between 'this is free and open' and 'I can actually use it today.'

The official options — Python CLI and DuckDB against S3 — are powerful but assume a developer environment most spatial analysts and planners don't have ready to go.

I built the Overture Downloader to close that gap: a browser tool where you draw your area of interest, select your dataset type, and get a GeoJSON download. Same underlying Overture data, no terminal required.

Comparison breakdown in the video. Both tools are free — pick the one that matches your workflow.
